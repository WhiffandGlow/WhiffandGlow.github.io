Shipping Policy

1. Processing Time
Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days of being placed. During peak seasons, such as holidays, processing times may be slightly longer. You will receive an email notification with tracking information once your order has shipped.

2. Shipping Rates
Shipping rates are calculated based on the shipping destination. You will be able to see the shipping cost before you complete your checkout.

3. Shipping Carriers
We use a variety of shipping carriers to ensure that your order arrives safely and on time. These carriers may include but are not limited to:

* USPS
* UPS
* FedEx
* DHL

4. Delivery Times
Delivery times vary depending on the shipping carrier and the shipping destination. Most orders within Sri Lanka will arrive within 3-5 business days. International orders may take longer.

5. Shipping Insurance
All orders are shipped with insurance. This insurance protects your order against loss or damage during transit.

6. Returns
We accept returns for unused and unopened candles within 30 days of purchase. Please contact us for a return authorization number before sending your return.

7. International Shipping
We ship internationally to select countries. Please contact us for more information on international shipping rates and restrictions.

8. Customs and Duties
You may be responsible for paying customs and duties when your order arrives in your country. These fees are not included in the shipping cost.

9. Damaged or Lost Shipments
If your order arrives damaged or lost, please contact us immediately. We will work with you to file a claim with the shipping carrier and ensure that you receive a replacement or refund.

10. Changes to Shipping Policy
We reserve the right to change our shipping policy at any time without notice. Please check this page periodically for updates.

11. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our shipping policy, please contact us at whiffandglow@gmail.com
